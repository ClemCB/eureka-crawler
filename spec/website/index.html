Welcome to Cat & Dog Facts

Cat Facts:

Cats are the most popular pet in the United States: There are 88 million pet cats and 74 million dogs. There are cats who have survived falls from over 32 stories (320 meters) onto concrete. A group of cats is called a clowder. Cats have over 20 muscles that control their ears. Cats sleep 70% of their lives.

Dog Facts:

Dogs

The reason dogs curl up is because of an age-old instinct to keep themselves warm and to protect vital organs while they sleep. Your dog's whiskers help him "see" in the dark. Dogs only have sweat glands in their paws.
